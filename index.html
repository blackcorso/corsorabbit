Big Bad Dog

A tense and frustrating Dog vs Rabbit game

Play Now How to Play

Corso vs Rabbit Game is the latest version of the hit survival tense and frustrating game that puts your survival skills to the test. Navigate through a big headed dog, collect carrots, and jump & run to stay alive in this thrilling adventure.

How to Play

Movement Controls

Mouse click or tap to jump and running.

Resource Collection

Collect resources by approaching carrot. These can be used for boost your energy to survive

Combat System

Click or tap as fast as possible to avoid the big headed dog that will eat you

Crafting & Upgrades

Open the inventory with Tab key. Combine items to craft weapons, tools, and survival equipment.

Game Role

You play as a rabbit being chased by a fierce dog with a big head. Run as fast as you can to avoid the big head dog that will eat you. Catch carrots to increase your energy
